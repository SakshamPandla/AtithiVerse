Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about traveling in India

The best time to visit India depends on the region. Generally, October to March offers pleasant weather across most of the country. Summer (April-June) is hot but great for hill stations, while monsoon (July-September) brings lush landscapes but heavy rains.

Most international visitors need a visa to enter India. We recommend applying for an e-Visa online, which is available for tourism, business, and medical purposes. Processing usually takes 2-4 business days.

India is generally safe for tourists when you follow basic precautions. We provide 24/7 support, work with trusted local partners, and offer safety guidelines for all our travelers. Our expert guides ensure you have a safe and memorable experience.

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels for your safety.
